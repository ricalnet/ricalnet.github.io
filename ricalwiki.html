RicalNet Wiki

RicalWiki is built on MediaWiki and operates solely on a local network for use in "Internet Offline" projects and as a learning module for communities in remote and underdeveloped areas, with a focus on general learning, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things.

If you'd like to obtain it , I'd be happy to provide the entire database and all it's contents.

Purpose

The project aims to provide educational resources in areas with limited internet access, bringing knowledge to communities that would otherwise have difficulty accessing such information.

Inspiration

This project is inspired by the idea of Mr. Onno Widodo Purbo , an Indonesian telecommunications expert known for his work in promoting internet access in developing regions.
